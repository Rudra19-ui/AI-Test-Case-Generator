# Healthcare Software Requirements

REQ-001: The system shall provide a secure login mechanism with multi-factor authentication for all healthcare providers.

REQ-002: The system shall encrypt all patient data at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3 or higher.

REQ-003: The system shall maintain an audit log of all access to patient records, including timestamp, user ID, and actions performed.

REQ-004: The system shall allow authorized users to view a patient's complete medical history in chronological order.

REQ-005: The system shall support the import and export of patient data in FHIR-compliant format.

REQ-006: The system shall automatically log out inactive users after 15 minutes of inactivity.

REQ-007: The system shall provide role-based access control to restrict access to patient information based on user role (e.g., physician, nurse, administrator).

REQ-008: The system shall allow patients to access their own medical records through a secure patient portal.

REQ-009: The system shall generate alerts for potential medication interactions when new medications are prescribed.

REQ-010: The system shall support electronic prescription functionality with digital signature capabilities compliant with DEA requirements for controlled substances.
